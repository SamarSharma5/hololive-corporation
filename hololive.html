// Who We Are

India's Trusted Name
In Holographic Security

HOLOLIVE CORPORATIONS LTD. is a leading manufacturer of hologram security stickers and tamper-evident labels, based in Sahibabad, Uttar Pradesh. We serve businesses across FMCG, pharmaceuticals, electronics, textiles, government documents, and luxury goods.

Our holograms are engineered with multi-layer optical security features — including 2D/3D effects, dot-matrix patterns, kinetic imagery, and nano-text — making them virtually impossible to duplicate with standard equipment.

Every label we produce is a shield for your brand — preserving consumer trust and safeguarding revenue from the growing threat of counterfeiting.

// How It Works

Our Production Process

01
Consultation & Design Brief

Share your brand assets, security requirements, and label dimensions. Our team creates a hologram concept exclusively for your use.

02
Master Origination

Using electron-beam lithography, we engrave your hologram pattern onto a master nickel shim — the foundation of every label produced.

03
Embossing & Metallisation

The pattern is hot-embossed onto PET film and vacuum-metallised for the brilliant mirror finish and light-diffracting holographic effect.

04
Converting & Serialisation

Labels are die-cut to shape, laminated with custom adhesive, and optionally printed with unique serial numbers, QR codes, or barcodes.

05
Quality Check & Dispatch

Every batch passes optical inspection and adhesion testing before secure, tamper-proof packaging and dispatch from Sahibabad.
